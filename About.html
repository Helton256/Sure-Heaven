About Sure Heaven Ministries

Spreading the Gospel and Building Churches Worldwide

Who We Are

Sure Heaven Ministries is a Christ-centered organization committed to transforming lives through faith, love, and service. Founded on Biblical principles, we are dedicated to spreading the Gospel, building churches, and empowering communities across the world.

Our Mission

To bring the light of the Gospel to every corner of the world by establishing churches, nurturing believers, and serving humanity through outreach and education.

Our Vision

To see a world transformed by the love of Christ — where every believer walks in faith, hope, and purpose for the glory of God.

Our Core Values

Faith

We depend on God’s word and promises in all we do.

Love

We serve others with the love of Christ and compassion for all.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and accountability in ministry.

Service

We dedicate our gifts and resources to building God’s Kingdom.
